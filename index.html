My Dogs

I'm only going to be showing you three of my dogs, Jill, Nessie, and Seamus.

    2. We found Jill with her brother, Jack, in an animal shelter. She lived a happy life and passed away earlier this year. She was a great dog who loved to sniff. She'd sit at the door and just sniff whatever smells come under it all night.

    3. Nessie is undoubtedly my dog. She's a border collie, and decided I was the sole member of her herd to follow and protect. I'm not around much anymore, so she tends to just make sure Seamus isn't getting in trouble.

  3. Seamus

    4. Seamus is a big golden retriever. He's still a puppy, and loves to play, chew, and swim. He's a lot bigger now than he was in the picture.
